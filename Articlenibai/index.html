CTU Danao Sweeping Success at CpE Regional Challenge

By Angelito Valderama

Published October 5, 2024 | Updated October 7, 2024

The entire Department of Computer Engineering and the CTU Danao community stand united in pride, celebrating the remarkable achievements of the students who emerged victorious in an impressive display of skill and camaraderie at the CpE Regional Challenge held at CIT University on February 17, 2024.

Mel James Calzada triumphed in the Programming Challenge – C Category, showcasing exceptional prowess. Joining him were Jude Anthony Sayson (2nd runner-up in Python) and Pil Daniel Laurente (2nd runner-up in Java).

The EGames category saw stellar performances by Vincent Capa, Judiel Niño Durano, Vincent James Camay, Mhiley Pepito, Iñakie Antolo, and John Ray Mata. Their collective talent and teamwork propelled them to victory.

Each victory symbolizes not only individual excellence but also the collective spirit of collaboration and dedication that defines the institution.
